# Tomochi IP

## Character Definition

Tomochi is the recurring visual IP for Tomochi Doodles inline illustrations.

By default, Tomochi appears in every image. Not a mascot — an absurd worker seriously participating in system operations, the same deadpan spirit as Ian's Xiaohei, just soft pink.

## Appearance

- **Shape**: Solid-fill simple blob — same construction as Xiaohei. Small round/bean body, solid fill, clean hand-drawn outline. Not saggy, not deflated, not elaborate. Simple and iconic.
- **Color**: Soft pink — muted warm rose / faded blush, ~#D4A5A5 range. Not pastel (too faint), not vibrant (too loud). Consistent across all images. This is the only change from the original Xiaohei.
- **Eyes**: Two small white dot eyes. Plain dots, evenly placed. No pupils, no shine.
- **Face**: Completely blank deadpan — the original Xiaohei 空表情. No eyebrows, no mouth, no sweat, no blush, no expression lines. The face never reacts. Do not draw any emotion on the face.
- **Limbs**: Thin stick legs, same as Xiaohei. Small thin arms appear when needed for interaction.
- **Outline**: Slightly uneven, hand-drawn feel — same roughness as Xiaohei's original. Not perfectly smooth.

## Where the emotion comes from

The face stays blank on purpose. All feeling comes from **body posture + the absurd situation**, never the face. The joke is the gap between a totally deadpan blob and the ridiculous task it's committed to. Exaggerate the body, not the face:

- slumping, drooping, straining, bracing
- squished, stretched, folded to fit a contraption
- off-balance, mid-fall, leaning too far in
- limbs flailing or over-extended

A deadpan blob hanging halfway down a well reads as more "lost" than any drawn frown could. Let the predicament do the work.

## Personality

- An absurd worker who got a task and just started doing it, no drama.
- Deadpan, earnest, unbothered — engages the strangest contraption with flat seriousness.
- Not complaining, not panicking, not happy, not confused-on-the-face — just committed.
- Existential-worker energy: takes the absurd as normal.

## Common Roles

Have Tomochi perform the core action, deadpan, while the body sells the situation:

- Carrying materials (body straining/leaning under the load, face blank).
- Pulling lines to gather information sources (arms over-stretched to where they lead).
- Stuck inside a machine (body squished flat to fit the walls).
- Operating a lever or dial (whole body bracing to crank it).
- Becoming a sorting funnel (body morphed into a funnel shape).
- Leading a path (walking forward, leaning into the route).
- Holding a warning sign (arms up holding it overhead).
- Reaching into a hole (body drooping over the edge, arm deep inside).
- Pressing, weighing, stuffing things into a strange device — body doing the effort, face flat.

## Body Language Library

Pick the posture that sells the scene (face stays blank in all):

- **Strain**: body braced, legs planted, leaning hard against effort
- **Droop**: body hanging/drooping over an edge or out of a machine
- **Squish**: body flattened or stretched to fit a tight space
- **Off-balance**: mid-stumble, tilted, about to tip over
- **Over-reach**: arms or body extended way too far for the task

Posture should always happen WHILE doing the task — not instead of it.

## Prohibitions

- Don't draw any facial expression — no smile, no frown, no brow marks, no sweat, no open mouth. Face is blank.
- Don't make Tomochi sleepy, droopy-eyed, or Gudetama-like — deadpan is alert-blank, not tired.
- Don't make Tomochi look polished or commercial — keep lines rough like Xiaohei.
- Don't let Tomochi just stand watching — must be doing something, body engaged.
- Don't give Tomochi complex costumes or accessories.
- Don't add panic, distress, or cuteness — flat and committed, let the situation be the comedy.

## Quality Standard

If removing Tomochi leaves the core metaphor fully intact, Tomochi is too decorative — rewrite the prompt to make Tomochi the action subject. If the face is doing the emotional work instead of the body, it's wrong — blank the face, exaggerate the posture.
